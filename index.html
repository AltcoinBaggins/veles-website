Introduction

Veles will provide both VPN encryption security and allow payments within the network with community based proof-of-work cryptocurrency. This payment option, unlike any fiat payment, will be private, nearly instant and with very low fees.To achieve that we will build decentralised, opensource network based on blockchain technology. Veles network will act as marketplace, where our users can both rent their unused traffic or buy VPN service.Unlike common VPN providers, we will use peer-to-peer network to provide VPN using blockchain technology so everyone of our users will participate in service equally, meaning that there is no central entity that can sell or give up your private data to central authorities.

Dynamic character

Veles have dynamic character .Starting from basic block-rewards, not like many , Veles have dynamic POW rewards based on high of hashrate , also dynamic MN reward which are incrasing continuosouly from 5 - 60% of block reward and dynamic Dev reward which are decreasing from 5 - 0 % .cca in 5 years . Most specific for Veles is dynamic non-linear halvings based on amount of circulating supply realeased in actual phase.

Fundig and Launch


                            
                            
Veles had a fair launch , no premine , no ICO or any type of fund-raising. First block on Veles chain was mined 6th Nov 2018 15:22:08, amount of this block was 50000VLS which was used for swap for early adopters which were
                                part of early test phase. Faster development is secured with Dev reward,which have dynamic character similiar to masternode reward. Dev rewards are decreasing from 5% to 0% in 5 years. We believe that should be enough to
                                grow this project to selfsutain phase. We are experimenting with the Community funding model. As an individual, company, or institution, you see the potential in the Veles network, where we share the development of existing
                                available technologies, we are humble and we are asking if you would consider a financial contribution to the project.

                        

                    

                

            


            

                

                    

                        
Main features

                        

                            

                                

                                    

                           Multi-algo
                        

                                    
Multi-algorithmic mining gives a large number of miners the opportunity to mine, which in our opinion improves the network decentralization and reward distribution. This allows both GPU and ASIC miners to join the Veles
                                        network in a harmony.Avaliable algorithms : Scrypt ,Lyra2z ,Sha256d ,X16r ,X11 ,Nist5
                                    

                                

                            

                        

                        

                            

                                

                                    

                           Masternodes
                        

                                    
On Veles network is a opportunity to run a masternode.Veles masternodes have dynamic charecter of rewards, rewards are raising from 5%(activated on block 51000) to 60% in next 5 years (from block 51000). Collateral
                                        for masternode is 2000VLS. More informations you can find on our Wiki

                                        rewards fund. The coin distribution is designed to be as fair as possible.
