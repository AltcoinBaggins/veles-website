What is Veles ?
Veles is a consensus network that enables a new payment system and a completely digital money. It is the decentralized peer-to-peer payment network that is powered by its users with no central authority or middlemen. From a user perspective, Veles is pretty much like cash for the Internet.
Who controls the Veles network ?
Nobody owns the Veles network much like no one owns the technology behind email. Veles is controlled by all Veles users around the world. While developers are improving the software, they can't force a change in the Veles protocol because all users are free to choose what software and version they use. In order to stay compatible with each other, all users need to use software complying with the same rules. Veles can only work correctly with a complete consensus among all users. Therefore, all users and developers have a strong incentive to protect this consensus.
How does Veles work ?
From a user perspective, Veles is nothing more than a mobile app or computer program that provides a personal Veles wallet and allows a user to send and receive VLS with them. This is how Veles works for most users. Behind the scenes, the Veles network is sharing a public ledger called the "block chain". This ledger contains every transaction ever processed, allowing a user's computer to verify the validity of each transaction. The authenticity of each transaction is protected by digital signatures corresponding to the sending addresses, allowing all users to have full control over sending VLS from their own Veles addresses. In addition, anyone can process transactions using the computing power of specialized hardware and earn a reward in VLS for this service. This is often called "mining".
When was Veles created ?
Veles was created/launched 13.11.2018.
Is Veles a private company and how is it funded ?
No, it is 100% open-source software and not a private company funded through an ICO or pre-mining.We believe that development and marketing effort will be supported by thousands of volunteer community members.
How Can I Get Veles coins ?
There are a few different ways to get Veles coins. The most common option is buying them on a supported exchange. Another way Veles is minable coin so you can participate on mining process and getting rewarded
Did Veles have a pre-mine?
No, Veles was not pre-mined. The development team and contributors purchase the coins on the open market like anyone else. A premine is a funding mechanism, whereby the developers of a currency allocate a certain amount to go to a particular address before releasing it to the public.
