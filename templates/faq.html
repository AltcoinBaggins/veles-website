Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently asked questions :
#1 What is Veles ?
Veles is a consensus network that aims to implement decentralized VPN. It's ecosystem also a decentralized peer-to-peer payment network that is powered by its users with no central authority or middlemen. From a user perspective, Veles is pretty much like cash for the Internet.
#2 Who controls the Veles network ?
Nobody owns the Veles network. Veles is controlled by all Veles users around the world. While developers are improving the software, they can't force a change in the Veles protocol because all users are free to choose what software and version they use. In order to stay compatible with each other, all users need to use software complying with the same rules. Veles can only work correctly with a complete consensus among all users. Therefore, all users and developers have a strong incentive to protect this consensus.
#3 How does Veles work ?
From a user perspective, Veles is nothing more than a mobile app or computer program that provides a personal Veles wallet and allows a user to send and receive VLS with them. This is how Veles works for most users. Behind the scenes, the Veles network is sharing a public ledger called the "block chain". This ledger contains every transaction ever processed, allowing a user's computer to verify the validity of each transaction. The authenticity of each transaction is protected by digital signatures corresponding to the sending addresses, allowing all users to have full control over sending VLS from their own Veles addresses. In addition, anyone can process transactions using the computing power of specialized hardware and earn a reward in VLS for this service. This is often called "mining".

#9 What is Veles mission ?
Veles aims to be fully decentralized, peer-to-peer based and serverless node network, designed to provide privacy restoring techniques to its users with financial incentivization to its node operators (providers).Veles achieves this goal by utilizing already-existing technologies such as blockchain and masternodes. Once Veles network is developed and released, it will protect user privacy and data, while enabling all users to share their spare bandwidth access with those who need it, in exchange for financial compensation. VPN service provided by the Veles network will be comparable with, and may even improve upon, existing Centralized Virtual Private Networking services. Veles market model will result in creating a VPN service which is both competitive and almost infinitely scalable, giving other entities an option to buy VPN service from the Veles network, integrating it into their solutions. This competitiveness comes from the open nature of the network and the ease with which anyone can earn money by joining it as a VPN service provider. and also to empower people around the world by bringing blockchain transactions into everyday life. Veles currency makes it possible to engage in direct transactions quickly, efficiently and privately on acceptable level for common users. With Veles currency, businesses and individuals have flexible options for sending and receiving payments. With the flip of a switch, we offer helpful integrations and tools that enable them to handle large scale transactions between merchants and small scale private payments.
