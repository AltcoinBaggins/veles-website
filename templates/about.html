About

VELES is yes, a technology, however with that, it’s also much more that just bits of code and elaborate algorithms. It’s a community. It’s an organically growing network of individuals assembling a set of core principals, beliefs, visions, and ideas, collaborating together to develop Veles. We are, at the end of the day, a collective of human beings coming together, inputting our various energies, resources, talents, and visions. Some of us are expert coders, ethical hackers. Others are good in managing community, web-development and more . As a reminder, often in tech and crypto realms, individuals are known by their “handles” (think nicknames) more than their own names.Veles is also opensource technology so you can see also you can participate on process of development.
