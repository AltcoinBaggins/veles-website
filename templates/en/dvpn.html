Decentralized VPN

The Goal

Veles Core is an open-source software project that aims to help the people of Internet to retain their freedom of access to an information and to improve privacy of the communications over the Internet by building decentralzied service based on VPN and multi-hop technology.
Taking advantage of the recent developents in the blockchain technology, related consensus mechanisms and concepts we're able to solve several issues from which current open anoymisation services suffer, which they are either commercial or based solely on volunteer networks. Making it a great deal more difficult and expensive for a bad actors or oppresive governments to disrupt the service or to invade one's privacy.
It also shows us how quickly a network can grow and progress and how well are people able to cooperate if every resources and effort are awarded fairly. Anyone can participate in the network in several roles, from miners, masternode operators, or people getting paid for their unused traffic.

Block rewards on Veles Network are dynamic, matching hashrate of the algo. Unlike in traditional PoW systems, on Veles Network is not possible to mine low-cost coins, and get full block reward when the hashrate would be low.
However, the dynamic block reward is limited by Maximum block reward, to prevent unexpected inflation and not to exceed planned supply release, reached only with sufficient hashrate. Minimum block would attract miners in case the hashrate of certain algo would get too low.
We have decided to use our concept non-linear, decreasing halving frewuency to prevent unneccessary long inflation period during the full PoW launch, but still keeping slow but steady release of VLS tokens for miners for over a century.
This creates supply curve and inflation rate more friendly to an investor, where
• over 50% of the max. supply will be in circulation within 2 years,
• over 70% of the max. supply within 5 years
• the rest will be released slowly in more than century time
