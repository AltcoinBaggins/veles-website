About

Veles is technology, however with that, it’s also much more that just bits of code and elaborate algorithms. It’s a community. It’s an organically growing network of individuals assembling a set of core principals, beliefs, visions, and ideas, collaborating together to develop Veles. We are, at the end of the day, a collective of human beings coming together, inputting our various energies, resources, talents, and visions. Some of us are expert coders, ethical hackers, others are good in managing community, web-development and more . As a reminder, often in tech and crypto realms, individuals are known by their “nicknames” more than their own names.
